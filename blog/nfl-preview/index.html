2026 NFL Season Preview: Key Matchups Every Fan Should Watch

The 2026 NFL season is shaping up to be one of the most exciting in recent memory. With new quarterback moves, emerging dynasties, and a competitive draft class, fans have plenty to look forward to. AFC Conference Outlook

The AFC remains stacked with talent. The Kansas City Chiefs continue to lead the charge behind their elite offense, while the Buffalo Bills and Cincinnati Bengals are hungry for a Super Bowl return. The Baltimore Ravens have retooled their receiving corps, and the Miami Dolphins boast one of the fastest offenses in the league. In the AFC South, the Jacksonville Jaguars and Houston Texans are both on the rise with young quarterbacks leading the way. The Denver Jets and Las Vegas Raiders are looking to bounce back after disappointing campaigns.

NFC Conference Outlook

The NFC is wide open. The San Francisco 49ers and Philadelphia Eagles remain the teams to beat after deep playoff runs. The Dallas Cowboys have retooled their defense and are aiming for a conference title. The Detroit Lions are coming off a breakthrough season and look ready to compete with the best. In the NFC South, the Atlanta Falcons and New Orleans Saints are in rebuilding mode, while the Chicago Bears and Green Bay Packers are both trending upward in the NFC North. The Los Angeles Rams and Seattle Seahawks round out a competitive NFC West.

Key Regular Season Matchups

This season features several must-watch games. The Chiefs travel to Buffalo in Week 6 for a rematch of recent playoff classics. The 49ers face the Eagles in Week 8 in a battle of NFC heavyweights. The Cowboys host the Giants on Thanksgiving Day in a rivalry that never disappoints. The Bengals take on the Ravens twice this season in what should be two hard-fought AFC North battles. The Super Bowl rematch between the Chiefs and 49ers is scheduled for Week 12 and could have major playoff seeding implications.
